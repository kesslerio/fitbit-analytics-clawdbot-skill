# Privacy Policy for Niemand Assistant

**Effective Date:** January 20, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Niemand Assistant ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with the Fitbit API integration.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following data from your Fitbit account:
*   **Activity Data:** Steps, distance, calories burned, active minutes.
*   **Heart Rate Data:** Continuous heart rate, resting heart rate.
*   **Sleep Data:** Sleep duration, stages, and efficiency.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data solely for:
*   **Personal Health Analytics:** Generating daily and weekly health reports.
*   **Automation:** Triggering smart home actions based on health metrics (e.g., sleep mode).
*   **Personal Insight:** Providing context-aware suggestions via the Niemand AI interface.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
*   **No Third-Party Sharing:** Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
*   **Internal Processing:** Data is processed locally on your personal server (Clawdbot) and via the Niemand AI assistant context.

## 5. Data Retention
Data is retained only as long as necessary for the personal analysis purposes described above. You may request deletion of your data at any time by clearing the local cache.
